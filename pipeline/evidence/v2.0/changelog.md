### Preserved Principles from v1.0

**Descriptive, Not Prescriptive:**
- All content remains descriptive research
- No Prisma color assignments
- No film rankings or scoring
- Illustrative examples only

**Ambiguity Preservation:**
- Contradictions maintained where sources disagree
- Cultural variations documented
- Context-dependent meanings preserved

**Source Attribution:**
- All claims tied to documented sources
- Academic and industry references maintained
- Primary source prioritization

### Limitations and Scope

**Cultural Representation:**
- Despite expansion, Western academic sources still dominant

- Translation and cultural interpretation challenges

**Temporal Coverage:**
- Contemporary digital developments may date quickly
- Historical coverage dependent on available documentation
- Some periods better documented than others

**Technical Evolution:**
- Rapid technological change may affect contemporary analysis
- New technologies and techniques continuously emerging
- Industry practice evolution ongoing
